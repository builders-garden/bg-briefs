What it does

✦

Daily Free Packs

Get one free pack daily plus premium pack purchases with 5 cards per pack. Build your collection over time with a rarity system spanning Common to Legendary tiers.

🎮

3D Card Gallery

Interactive 3D card viewer with flip animations and holographic effects for legendary cards. Browse your collection with touch-triggered video playback and stunning visual effects.

💸

Marketplace Trading

List cards for sale with fixed pricing, purchase cards from other collectors, and discover rare finds. 5% platform fee supports the community and ongoing development.

👥

Social Features

Connect with friends, browse their collections, and share your best card pulls. Build a community around your shared passion for vaping culture and card collecting.

📱

Cross-Platform PWA

Seamless experience across mobile and desktop with account sync, social login options, and integrated payment processing for premium content.
